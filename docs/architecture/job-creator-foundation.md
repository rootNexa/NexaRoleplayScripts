# Job Creator Foundation

Der spaetere Job Creator erstellt Definitionen, Phasen, Tasks, Resource Nodes, Production Chains, Rewards und Cooldowns ingame.

Permissions:

- `nexa.jobs.view`
- `nexa.jobs.create`
- `nexa.jobs.update`
- `nexa.jobs.activate`
- `nexa.jobs.suspend`
- `nexa.jobs.disable`
- `nexa.jobs.nodes.manage`
- `nexa.jobs.rewards.manage`
- `nexa.jobs.audit.view`
